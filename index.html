Welcome to my site! My name is Jordan. I am a programmer with a passion for developing video games. My primary area of interest is mixed reality, specifically virtual reality. I am currently a programmer for VR Rehab, a company that offers military solutions involving robotics and mixed reality. On the side I am helping develop a VR game called Immersion Sector with a small team of friends.

I am a self-taught Unity3D / C# developer and have been working in Unity for over 8 years now. I have a passion for teaching aspiring game developers how to make games in Unity. If you are interested in utilizing my instructing services, please click here.

I also have a YouTube channel called jlo-vr. There I upload content related to virtual reality game development. Visit the Projects page on this site if you want to see what programming and DIY projects I've worked on. You can also check out my Blog if you want to see some of my writings!
